TITLE:
THE COST OF DISTRACTION

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Recovery begins with attention.

The absence of attention produces a different outcome.

Distraction.

Most losses occur long before they are noticed.

Opportunities disappear.

Lessons pass.

Relationships weaken.

Patterns emerge unnoticed.

Not because they were hidden.

Because attention never arrived.

The Archive recognizes distraction as one of the primary obstacles to recovery.

A distracted observer may possess knowledge.

A distracted observer may possess intelligence.

Yet neither guarantees awareness.

Presence reveals.

Distraction conceals.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

What is ignored is rarely recovered.
