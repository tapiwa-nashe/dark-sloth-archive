ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Not everything that is seen is understood.

Not everything that is understood is seen clearly.

The Archive recognizes this distinction through the Rune of Clarity.

Clarity is the sixth and final Rune of Observation.

Clarity teaches the seeker to see.

Not what is desired.

Not what is feared.

What is.

Clarity emerges from all five preceding runes.

Presence creates attention.

Patience sustains attention.

Silence deepens attention.

Discernment organizes attention.

Humility protects attention.

Clarity emerges from all five.

Clarity should never be confused with certainty.

Clarity sees accurately.

Certainty believes completely.

Clarity remains open.

Certainty closes.

The shadow of Clarity is Certainty.

Seek clarity, not certainty.

One deepens recovery.

The other ends it.
