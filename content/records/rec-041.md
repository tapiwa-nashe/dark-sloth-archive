TITLE:
REMAINING LONG ENOUGH

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Many people leave before meaning appears.

They arrive.

Observe briefly.

Judge quickly.

Depart.

Patience teaches a different approach.

Remain.

Remain through uncertainty.

Remain through ambiguity.

Remain through silence.

Recovery often requires duration.

Meaning emerges slowly.

The observer who remains gains access to layers unavailable to those who rush.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

Some truths reveal themselves only to those who stay.
