TITLE:
CERTAINTY AS BLINDNESS

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Certainty presents itself as strength.

Often it is blindness.

The observer who becomes certain stops looking.

Stops listening.

Stops questioning.

Stops recovering.

Humility protects against this condition.

Not by reducing confidence.

By preserving openness.

Recovery requires the possibility of surprise.

The possibility of correction.

The possibility of discovery.

Certainty removes all three.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

The observer who cannot be surprised can no longer recover.
