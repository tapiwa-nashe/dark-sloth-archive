TITLE:
SIGNIFICANCE AND WEIGHT

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Not every fragment carries equal significance.

Not every signal deserves preservation.

The Archive therefore studies Weight.

Weight is not popularity.

Weight is not visibility.

Weight is not volume.

Weight is significance.

A thing possesses Weight when its consequences extend beyond itself.

Discernment allows the observer to recognize Weight.

Without Discernment, attention becomes scattered.

With Discernment, recovery becomes focused.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

Significance rarely announces itself.
