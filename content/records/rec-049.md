TITLE:
THE WEIGHT OF SMALL THINGS

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Many observers search for significance in dramatic events.

The Archive teaches another lesson.

Small things often carry tremendous Weight.

A brief conversation.

A forgotten note.

A passing observation.

A seemingly insignificant choice.

Discernment reveals that significance and visibility are not the same thing.

The observer learns to recognize Weight wherever it appears.

Not merely where attention gathers.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

The smallest fragment may carry the largest consequence.
