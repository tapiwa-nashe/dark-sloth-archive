TITLE:
WAITING WITHOUT WITHDRAWAL

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Patience is frequently mistaken for inactivity.

The Archive recognizes a difference.

Withdrawal disconnects.

Patience remains engaged.

The patient observer remains present.

Remains attentive.

Remains available.

The world continues unfolding.

Patience allows participation in that unfolding.

Patience remains connected to what it awaits.
