TITLE:
THE DISCIPLINE OF ATTENTION

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Attention is often treated as a resource.

The Archive treats it as a discipline.

Resources are consumed.

Disciplines are cultivated.

Presence does not emerge accidentally.

It is practiced.

Attention returns.

Again.

And again.

And again.

The observer learns to notice when attention wanders.

The observer learns to return.

Recovery deepens through repetition.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

Attention grows where it is repeatedly returned.
