TITLE:
CLARITY WITHOUT CERTAINTY

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Many people pursue certainty.

The Archive pursues clarity.

The distinction matters.

Certainty seeks conclusions.

Clarity seeks accuracy.

Certainty closes.

Clarity remains open.

A clear observer can continue learning.

A certain observer rarely does.

Recovery deepens when clarity expands.

Recovery ends when certainty becomes absolute.

The Archive therefore teaches:

Seek clarity.

Remain open.

Continue observing.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

Clarity deepens recovery. Certainty concludes it.
