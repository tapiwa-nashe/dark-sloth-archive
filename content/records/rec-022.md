ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Many people seek answers.

Few remain long enough to receive them.

Patience is the Second Rune of Observation.

If Presence teaches the seeker to arrive,

Patience teaches the seeker to remain.

Patience is not passive waiting.

Patience is sustained engagement.

The Archive teaches that reality unfolds according to rhythms larger than preference.

The patient observer participates in them.

Patience emerges from the interaction of Void and Weight.

Void provides space.

Weight provides significance.

Together they create meaningful endurance.

The shadow of Patience is Stagnation.

Where Patience remains engaged,

Stagnation withdraws.

Patience remains connected to recovery.

Stagnation disconnects from it.

Remain.

But remain purposefully.

Observe.

But continue participating.

Wait.

But remain alive to what is unfolding.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

Do not ask how quickly meaning appears.

Ask whether you remained long enough to receive it.
