TITLE:
LISTENING BENEATH NOISE

ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Noise occupies attention.

Meaning occupies depth.

The observer who learns silence discovers another layer.

Beneath reaction.

Beneath urgency.

Beneath interruption.

Patterns remain.

Truth remains.

Meaning remains.

Silence does not create these things.

Silence allows them to be heard.

RECOVERED FRAGMENT:

What is deepest is often quietest.
