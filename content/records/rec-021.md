ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Before recovery can begin, something must be noticed.

Before meaning can be recovered, something must be encountered.

The Archive recognizes this condition as Presence.

Presence is the First Rune.

The doorway through which every recovery passes.

Many confuse Presence with proximity.

The Archive distinguishes between the two.

To be near something is not necessarily to be present with it.

Presence begins when attention arrives.

The world continuously presents fragments.

Moments.

Symbols.

Conversations.

Lessons.

Most pass unnoticed.

Not because they lack significance.

Because attention is elsewhere.

Presence emerges from the interaction of Void and Thread.

Void provides space.

Thread provides connection.

Too much connection without space becomes fixation.

Too much space without connection becomes detachment.

Presence balances both.

The Rune teaches attention as discipline.

Not mood.

Not emotion.

Not circumstance.

Attention.
