ARCHIVE ENTRY:

Not everything deserves equal attention.

Not every signal carries meaning.

Not every fragment requires preservation.

The Archive recognizes this reality through the Rune of Discernment.

Discernment teaches the seeker to recognize.

Without Discernment, attention becomes overwhelmed.

Observation becomes scattered.

Recovery becomes directionless.

The Rune emerges from Presence and Weight.

Presence provides attention.

Weight provides significance.

Together they create meaningful recognition.

The Archive teaches that significance rarely announces itself.

Many of the most meaningful recoveries appear ordinary at first.

The undiscerning observer passes over them.

The discerning observer notices their Weight.

The shadow of Discernment is Judgment.

Where Discernment seeks understanding,

Judgment seeks certainty.

Where Discernment remains curious,

Judgment becomes rigid.

Discernment should increase humility.

Not diminish it.
